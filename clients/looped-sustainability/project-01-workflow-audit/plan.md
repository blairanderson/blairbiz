# project-01-workflow-audit

## What
Document all major manual workflows across Looped's operations, identify automation opportunities, and produce a prioritized roadmap for the engagement.

## Why
Neither Blair nor Looped has a complete picture of every manual step. The audit produces the map — everything downstream depends on it. Cort and Andrew mentioned several pain points across intake, freight, AP, and sales that haven't been fully diagrammed.

## Scope
- Intake: how a new job arrives (email, phone call) and what happens next
- Research flow: how a material card moves from Trello to a qualified buyer list
- Freight quoting: full path from load specs to carrier selection
- AP/AR: full path from invoice receipt to QuickBooks posting
- Account management: scheduling, confirmations, document generation
- Sales: call → Trello card → follow-up

## Deliverable
Written workflow document for each process above, with time estimates per step and a prioritized automation opportunity list.
