# project-09-lead-list-builder

## What
Targeted contact list generation for the sales team — not email blasting, just building "who to call" lists by material type and geography. Example: "Give me 20 cattle farmers within 100 miles and their phone numbers."

## Why
Finding the right buyer for niche loads is relationship-dependent today (e.g., the Alabama bait-solution contact for 3M lbs/year of dead trout). Cold blasting unknown contacts doesn't work in this industry. They need warm, targeted leads — the right person to call first.

## Scope
- Input: material type + geography (radius or state)
- Output: ranked contact list with name, company, phone, and relevance notes
- Sources: public databases, web research, industry directories
- Integrates naturally with project-03 (research agent) — research agent finds buyers, this project builds the call list

## Notes
- Cort's exact framing: "finds our best options, gives us a pricing guide, and tees up exactly who we need to call."
- LinkedIn and ZoomInfo already used for outbound. This project supplements those with material-specific discovery.
- Output is a list for human callers — no automated outreach.
