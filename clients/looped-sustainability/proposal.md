## Initial Work Streams

Three entry points, sequenced by impact:

### 1. AP/AR Automation *(start here)*

An intake pipeline that reads the AP email inbox, extracts invoice data from attachments, validates amounts against C-Trade records, flags mismatches, and prepares QuickBooks postings — removing the manual reconciliation loop entirely. Fully internal. Nothing touches suppliers or clients. This is a workflow automation problem, not an AI problem — the right tool here is structured rules and triggers, not a language model.

### 2. Research Agent

An AI agent that watches Trello. When a new material card appears, it researches best end uses, surfaces nearby buyers and processors with direct contact info, and posts a prioritized "who to call" list with indicative pricing back onto the card. Replaces the 20-hour manual research cycle per lead with something that runs overnight.

### 3. Sales Call Transcription → Trello Enrichment

Background audio capture — no bot joins your call, nothing intrusive. Generates a call summary, extracts specs and open questions, and updates the relevant Trello card. Captures what your sales reps leave out. Coaching prompts ("did you ask about weight per pallet?") can follow.
