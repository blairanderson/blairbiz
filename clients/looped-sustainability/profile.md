## Pain Points
- Logistics coordination is manual and phone-heavy (dock instructions, security codes, facility shutdowns)
- Emergency quoting — need to blast all carriers within ~1 hour, done manually today
- R&D on unusual materials (e.g. 3M lbs/year dead trout) — slow, relies on ChatGPT ad hoc
- Finding the right buyers/outlets for unusual loads
- Manual data entry into CIETrade (no API, walled garden)
