## 1. Services

Contractor will provide fractional CTO services to Client on an ongoing basis. The engagement is structured as an ongoing advisory and implementation relationship — not tracked by the hour and not scoped project-by-project. Services include:

- Weekly check-ins with Client team to identify operational friction and prioritize automation opportunities
- Designing, building, and deploying software and automation solutions as agreed during working sessions
- Async availability via Slack or email for questions, review, and coordination
- Initial focus areas: accounts payable inbox automation, research agent for material outlet discovery, and sales call transcription with Trello enrichment

Automation approaches will be selected based on fit for the problem. AI will be applied where it provides meaningful advantage over simpler workflow solutions; structured automation, rules-based logic, and standard integrations will be used where they are more appropriate.

Specific deliverables, timelines, and priorities will be agreed upon collaboratively at the start of each work cycle. This agreement covers the ongoing relationship; individual project scopes do not require separate contracts.

## 2. Term

This agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of **three (3) months**. After the initial term, the agreement automatically renews on a **month-to-month** basis until terminated by either party in accordance with Section 7.

---

## 3. Compensation

Client will pay Contractor a flat monthly fee of **$4,500.00 USD**.

Contractor will issue an invoice on the first calendar day of each month. Payment is **due upon receipt**.

If payment is not received within ten (10) days of invoice, any unpaid balance will accrue interest at **1.5% per month (18% per annum)** until paid in full. Contractor may also suspend services until the outstanding balance is paid.

---

## 4. Independent Contractor

Contractor is an independent contractor, not an employee of Client. Contractor is responsible for all taxes on compensation received under this agreement. Contractor may perform services for other clients during the term of this agreement, provided doing so does not create a conflict of interest.

For clarity, Contractor serving other clients in adjacent industries does not constitute a conflict of interest unless Contractor directly uses Client's Confidential Information (as defined in Section 6) in performing services for a direct competitor of Client.

---

## 5. Work Product and Intellectual Property

All software, code, documentation, and workflows specifically created for Client under this agreement ("Work Product") are **owned by Client** upon receipt of full payment for the period in which they were created. Contractor assigns all rights, title, and interest in Work Product to Client, subject to the following:

**(a) Pre-existing and Generic IP.** Work Product may incorporate software, tools, templates, open-source components, or workflow patterns developed by Contractor prior to or independently of this engagement ("Contractor IP"). Contractor retains all ownership of Contractor IP. Contractor grants Client a perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use Contractor IP solely as embedded in or necessary to use the Work Product.

**(b) Open Source.** To the extent Work Product incorporates open-source components, those components remain subject to their applicable open-source licenses, which Client agrees to comply with.

**(c) Confidential Information.** Contractor's right to use general patterns and methodologies in other engagements does not include the right to use Client's Confidential Information (as defined in Section 6) in performing services for any third party.

Contractor retains the right to use general skills, knowledge, and know-how developed during the engagement for other work.

---

## 6. Confidentiality

Contractor agrees to keep confidential all non-public information about Client's business, customers, vendors, pricing, and operations ("Confidential Information"). This obligation survives termination of the agreement for a period of **two (2) years**.

Client agrees to keep confidential any proprietary methods, tools, or frameworks disclosed by Contractor that are not specific to Client's business.

These confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that: (i) is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this agreement; (ii) was known to the receiving party prior to disclosure; (iii) is independently developed by the receiving party without use of Confidential Information; or (iv) is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided the disclosing party is given prompt written notice to seek a protective order.

---

## 7. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with **30 days written notice** after the initial three-month term. During the initial term, either party may terminate for material breach if the breach is not cured within 10 business days of written notice.

The effective termination date shall be the last day of the calendar month following the calendar month in which notice is given (e.g., notice given April 15 results in a May 31 termination date). Fees through the effective termination date are payable in full.

Upon termination, Contractor will deliver all Work Product completed and paid for through the termination date.
