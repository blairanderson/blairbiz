# project-03-research-agent

## What
AI agent that watches Trello for new material cards, researches best end uses and potential buyers, and posts a structured comment directly on the card — with a ranked buyer list, indicative pricing, and freight estimates.

## Why
Research is eating 20 hours per lead. Sales reps are better at cold calling than research, so Tommy, Andrew, and Cort step in constantly. Cort's immediate favorite from first meeting — "lit up on this one." Lowest risk of all projects (entirely internal, no client/vendor exposure).

## Scope
- Trello webhook or polling integration on designated board
- Trigger: new card added (or label applied)
- Research: material type → likely end uses → nearby buyers/processors → contact info + indicative pricing
- Output: structured Trello comment with ranked options and "who to call" list
- Handles both common materials and unusual loads (dead trout, raw chicken, plastic liners)

## Notes
- Cort originally suggested trying getVictor.com first. This was the pre-engagement suggestion. Now that we're engaged, evaluate Victor vs. custom build — custom gives them control and handles niche materials better.
- API exposure concern was raised by Cort in first meeting: addressed — data stays inside their Trello, no competitor risk.
