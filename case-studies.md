## Invoice Processing Automation

**Problem:** A business processing hundreds of invoices per month through Bill.com was spending hours on manual data entry... moving due dates, validating amounts, and submitting forms one at a time.

**Approach:** Built a custom JavaScript automation that processes invoices in bulk. The script adjusts due dates to the nearest Monday, validates transaction amounts, and auto-submits forms with timed intervals to avoid rate limiting.

**Outcome:** What took hours of manual work now runs in minutes. The team handles the same volume with a fraction of the effort.

## Database Migration at Scale

**Problem:** A production Rails application needed to migrate from Heroku's managed PostgreSQL to a Hatchbox-hosted server without losing data or experiencing extended downtime.

**Approach:** Used pgsync for incremental data transfer over SSH, handled constraint conflicts, and validated data integrity across both environments before cutting over.

**Outcome:** Clean migration with data integrity preserved. The application runs on infrastructure that costs less and provides more control.

## E-Commerce Platform Management

**Problem:** Managing tens of millions of dollars in annual GMV across dozens of Amazon accounts, each with different brands, inventory levels, and compliance requirements.

**Approach:** Built internal tools and automation systems to handle catalog management, pricing optimization, and cross-account operations at scale. Developed fraud detection systems and SEO strategies specific to the Amazon marketplace.

**Outcome:** Grew the operation from a single account to managing $10M+ in annual GMV across 20+ accounts, with automated systems handling the complexity that would otherwise require a much larger team.
