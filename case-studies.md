## ShipmentBot — AI-Powered Amazon Automation

**Problem:** Amazon sellers managing FBA inventory spend hours on manual purchase orders, shipment planning, and demand forecasting. The process doesn't scale past a handful of ASINs without a team.

**What we built:** [ShipmentBot](https://www.shipmentbot.com) is a full automation platform for Amazon sellers. AI/ML demand forecasting predicts restock needs. Automated purchase order creation and shipment handling runs the entire FBA inbound process hands-free. Real-time inventory tracking with automated reordering keeps stock levels optimal.

**Result:** Thousands of Amazon sellers use ShipmentBot to manage their FBA operations. Sellers can go 100% hands-free or get daily optimization recommendations, handling the complexity that would otherwise require dedicated operations staff.

## BigDetailPage — Amazon Catalog Monitoring & Protection

**Problem:** Amazon sellers spend months optimizing their product listings, then competitors or unauthorized sellers silently modify the content. A+ materials disappear. Brand messaging gets changed. By the time you notice, you've lost sales.

**What we built:** [BigDetailPage](https://www.bigdetailpage.com) monitors Amazon listings in real time and alerts sellers the moment anything changes. Automated content version tracking lets you restore listings with one click. A centralized dashboard tracks brand consistency across your entire catalog, and competitive intelligence shows exactly what your competitors are changing and when.

**Result:** Sellers protect their catalog investment and catch unauthorized changes before they impact sales. Competitive monitoring reveals market strategy shifts in real time.

## RizzForms — Form Submission SaaS

**Problem:** Every website needs forms, but building and maintaining backend form processing is tedious infrastructure work. Developers waste time on spam protection, email delivery, webhook routing, and data storage instead of building their actual product.

**What we built:** [RizzForms](https://www.rizzness.com) lets you drop a standard HTML `<form>` tag on any site. We handle the rest: secure submission collection, spam filtering with honeypots and rate limiting, webhook forwarding to Slack/Zapier/custom APIs, email notifications, CSV export, and a full REST API. White-label domain support for agencies.

**Result:** Used by contractors, service providers, and indie developers. Free tier to $60/month. Blair.biz runs on RizzForms right now (the contact form on this site submits to our own platform).

## HydroseedCalculator — Vertical SaaS for an Analog Industry

**Problem:** The hydroseeding industry is stuck in the '90s. Getting a single quote takes days. Homeowners have no way to estimate costs without calling contractors and waiting for someone to show up with a tape measure.

**What we built:** [HydroseedCalculator](https://www.hydroseedcalculator.com) gives homeowners instant cost estimates using satellite imagery to measure their yard. No email signup required. A built-in sod vs. hydroseeding comparison tool helps with the decision. A searchable installer directory connects homeowners with local contractors across all 50 states.

**Result:** Brought a decades-old industry online. Homeowners get estimates in seconds instead of days. Contractors get qualified leads from people who already understand pricing.

## Invoice Processing Automation

**Problem:** A business processing hundreds of invoices per month through Bill.com was spending hours on manual data entry, moving due dates, validating amounts, and submitting forms one at a time.

**What we built:** A custom automation that processes invoices in bulk. The script adjusts due dates to the nearest Monday, validates transaction amounts, and auto-submits forms with timed intervals to avoid rate limiting.

**Result:** Hours of monthly manual work reduced to minutes. Same volume, fraction of the effort.
