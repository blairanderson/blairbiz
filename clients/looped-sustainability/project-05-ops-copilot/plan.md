# project-05-ops-copilot

## What
Workflow automation for the CS team: convert incoming requests into structured tasks, draft standard confirmations, track load status, and generate documents — reducing the phone-to-spreadsheet loop.

## Why
Two new CS reps were just hired. Account management is phone-heavy and error-prone: scheduling trucks, confirming dock instructions, tracking weights, generating documents — all done by humans in real time. This is the repeatable-task layer that automation absorbs best.

## Scope
- Structured intake from incoming emails/messages (parse load request details)
- Auto-generate draft confirmation messages for client approval
- Status tracking per load (scheduled, confirmed, in transit, complete)
- Document generation: standard BOL drafts, pickup confirmations, weight tickets
- Escalation alerts for unplanned shutdowns or missed confirmations

## Notes
- Must be invisible to clients — they said automation cannot change how suppliers and clients operate externally.
- The CS team is the internal user here. UX needs to be simple enough that a new hire can use it without training overhead.
