01 — AP / AR Invoice Automation

Andrew's #1 Priority

Monitors the AP email inbox. Extracts invoice data from attachments (PDFs, Excel). Validates amounts against C-Trade records. Flags mismatches. Prepares QuickBooks postings for review.

Andrew stops manually touching invoices. Discrepancies surface automatically rather than silently accumulating.

Why first: Lowest risk — fully internal, clients never see it. Highest daily pain for Andrew. Immediate, measurable relief.

"That's why Andrew doesn't sleep anymore."

Andrew Januik, COO
Deliverable 1 of 4 · Phase 1 Build

02 — Material Research Agent

Cort's Priority

Plugs into the Trello pipeline. When a new opportunity card is created, the agent identifies likely non-obvious end uses, surfaces nearby buyers and processors with contacts, and comments a prioritized "who to call" list — with indicative pricing — directly on the card.

Sales reps get a research head start before they ever pick up the phone.

Their framing, built exactly: "Finds our best options, gives us a pricing guide, and tees up exactly who we need to call."

"The pain point is the 20 hours that go into finding that phone call."

Cort Koenig, CEO
Deliverable 2 of 4 · Phase 1 Build

03 — Sales Call Transcription → Trello

Both Benefit

Background audio capture — Granola-style. No bot joining the call, nothing intrusive for the prospect. After each call, generates a structured summary: material specs, open questions, pricing signals, follow-up items. Written directly to the relevant Trello card.

Cards become as detailed as the ones Cort writes himself.

Coaching layer available: Prompts like "Did you ask about volume timing?" can reinforce the sales process over time.

Note: you flagged that you disliked intrusive AI meeting bots from a prior tool. This approach runs in the background — the prospect never knows.

Deliverable 3 of 4 · Phase 1 Build

04 — Workflow Audit & Automation Roadmap

Foundation

Document the major manual workflows end-to-end: freight quoting, account management, order tracking, document generation. Identify automation candidates by impact and risk.

Produces the prioritized roadmap that guides Retainer Phase work. We're never guessing what to build next.

Natural next layer: Freight quoting and account management ops are the Phase 2 targets. The audit makes sure we sequence them correctly.

  • Freight quoting — always-on coverage beyond Macedonia work window

  • Account management ops copilot — scheduling, confirmations, document generation
